Shepherd United Methodist Church to host “Back-to-School Bash” August 9

The Shepherd United Methodist Church will be hosting a “Back to School Bash” on Tuesday, August 9 from 5:30 - 7:30 p.m. at the Little Salt River Park for kids in grades K-5.

Here is what we know about the event based on the flyer:

  • A “Hot Dog Dinner” will be served
  • There will be free popcorn
  • There will be free Snow Cones
  • There will be music
  • School supplies will be given to the first 80 children who register
